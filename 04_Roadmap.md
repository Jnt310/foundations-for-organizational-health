# 04 — Roadmap

**Body of Work:** Foundations for Organizational Health™  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Draft  
**Date:** June 25, 2026  

## Purpose

The Roadmap outlines development phases for the Foundations for Organizational Health™ body of work.

## Strategic Direction

Foundations for Organizational Health™ will begin in behavioral healthcare, where the need is urgent and the founder’s expertise is strongest. Over time, the philosophy may expand to healthcare, human services, education, nonprofit organizations, and other helping professions.

## Development Phases

### Phase 0 — Foundation

**Status:** Active

Core deliverables:

- Foundations Charter
- Master Index
- Intellectual Property Register
- Version History
- Roadmap
- Research Agenda
- Culture of Organizational Healing
- Healthy Organizational Ecosystem

Primary goal:

Establish the philosophical, ethical, and governance foundation of the body of work.

### Phase 1 — Core Framework

**Status:** Planned

Core deliverables:

- Organizational Health Framework
- Healthy Organizational Ecosystem visual model
- Domain definitions
- Foundational conditions
- Leading and lagging indicator model
- Glossary

Primary goal:

Define what organizational health means and how the ecosystem functions.

### Phase 2 — Assessment System

**Status:** Planned

Core deliverables:

- Organizational Health Assessment
- Leadership Capacity Assessment
- Culture Assessment
- Workforce Sustainability Assessment
- Organizational Well-Being Index prototype
- Scoring methodology
- Interview guide
- Executive report template

Primary goal:

Create practical tools for diagnosing organizational health.

### Phase 3 — Evidence and Validation

**Status:** Future

Core deliverables:

- Literature review
- Research model
- Pilot testing plan
- Validation hypotheses
- Case study structure
- Data collection plan

Primary goal:

Move from philosophy and practice wisdom toward evidence-informed and eventually evidence-supported tools.

### Phase 4 — Consulting Toolkit

**Status:** Future

Core deliverables:

- Executive assessment package
- Service line development toolkit
- Financial sustainability model
- Culture intervention toolkit
- Leadership development toolkit
- Implementation roadmap templates
- Dashboard templates

Primary goal:

Translate the philosophy into reusable consulting assets.

### Phase 5 — Platform and Productization

**Status:** Future

Core deliverables:

- Interactive assessments
- Automated report generation
- Dashboard concepts
- Training curriculum
- Certification pathway
- Book or publication plan
- Conference/speaking materials

Primary goal:

Prepare the body of work for broader dissemination, teaching, licensing, and scalable impact.

## Current Next Priorities

1. Refine the Foundations Charter.
2. Build the Culture of Organizational Healing foundational paper.
3. Define the Healthy Organizational Ecosystem model.
4. Develop the first draft of organizational health domains.
5. Begin the research map for Organizational Well-Being.

## Version 0.1 — Ratification

**Date:** June 25, 2026  
**Status:** Ratified as Active Development  

### Major Decisions

1. The body of work will be called **Foundations for Organizational Health™** as the umbrella philosophy.
2. The central model will be developed as the **Healthy Organizational Ecosystem**.
3. Behavioral health will be the first application area, not the long-term boundary.
4. The long-term scope may include healthcare, human services, education, nonprofit organizations, and other helping professions.
5. The work will prioritize organizational health, culture, leadership, stewardship, workforce sustainability, ethics, learning, and sustainable care.
6. The project will maintain governance documents and version-controlled foundational papers.

### Legacy Statement Ratified

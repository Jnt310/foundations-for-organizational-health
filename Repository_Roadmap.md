# Founder's Charter (Working)

## Why
Help heal our systems so the people working within them can better heal those they entered this field to serve.

## Core Beliefs
- We do the hard work, even when it is uncomfortable.
- It is okay to be wrong; learning is how we improve.
- Every employee, every role, and every position is meaningful.
- No title makes one person's opinion inherently more valuable than another's.
- We strengthen weaknesses by leveraging strengths.
- Healthy organizations create the conditions for healthier people, healthier services, and healthier communities.

## Purpose

Foundations for Organizational Health™ is a living body of work designed to advance the science and practice of organizational health, beginning in behavioral healthcare and expanding over time to healthcare, human services, and other mission-driven helping professions.

The philosophy exists to help heal the systems that care for people so that the people within those systems can sustainably, ethically, and effectively care for others.

## Working Rule

Every substantive working session should end with a Methodology Update that identifies:

- Documents created or updated
- New concepts added
- Version changes
- Research questions added
- Open issues
- Recommended next priority

## Scope

Behavioral health is the first application area and proof environment. The broader philosophy is intended to apply to healthcare, human services, education, nonprofit, and other mission-driven organizations where people-centered work depends on healthy organizational conditions.
